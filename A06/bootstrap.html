MULTILINGUAL CHILDREN’S E-BOOK

The idea for this project began with my 7-year-old son who is a half-Korean and half-American. I teach him Korean and often read Korean books to him. But getting Korean children’s books in the US is quite a hassle. Therefore, I thought it would be nice if there was an app that provides children’s e-books in multiple languages. I created a brand named MODOO which means all, or everyone in Korean. This app provides children’s e-books in multiple languages on its app. Many high-quality children’s books have been well-translated and they are being read by children all over the world. This brand will provide services by collecting these books in one place. It’s also good for the environment because people don’t need physical books. This app’s target audience is multilingual families who have children aged Pre-school to Middle school. The ultimate goal of this service is to provide an easier way for bilingual or multilingual children to access a variety of books and let children know the joy of reading in multiple languages. Children who subscribe to this app for the first time will receive a welcome box. It contains tangible items such as a T-shirt, a phone grip, stickers, and a reading reward chart. On the app, children can make their own characters and the characters are printed on these items. Through these items, children will be able to feel more connected in using the app.
